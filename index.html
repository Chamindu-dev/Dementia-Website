How it Works

Our system uses a Deep Convolutional Neural Network trained on thousands of MRI scans from the OASIS dataset. It identifies subtle patterns of atrophy in the hippocampus and cerebral cortex that are indicative of early-stage dementia.

Classification Classes

  • Non Demented (CDR 0)
  • Very Mild Dementia (CDR 0.5)
  • Mild Dementia (CDR 1)
  • Moderate Dementia (CDR 2)

Research Background

Developed as part of a final year university research project exploring "AI in Neurodegenerative Disease Diagnosis". The model achieves 95% validation accuracy on the test set.
